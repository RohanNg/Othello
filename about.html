About us

Othello's bakery was founded in Vaasa, Finland by businessman Anders Hedman. The first bakery shop was inaugurated on December 17, 1956 in a new building at Palossarentie 18. In 1964, production increased Othello had five stores in the bakery. Company name Oy Othello-Products Ab was introduced in the 1980s in connection with the ownership change.
